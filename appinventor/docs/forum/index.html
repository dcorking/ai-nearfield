App Inventor for Android User Discussion Forum

Read and follow these IMPORTANT GUIDELINES as you participate in these forums. This is the way to make many friends on the forums!
  • You can join one or all of these sub-forums. Click on the title of the sub-forum to see forum details and join. Read FORUM TIPS for more information on the functionality of these forums.
  • Read the USER FAQ, the TROUBLESHOOTING page, and the App Inventor DOCUMENTATION before posting. Chances are good your question will be answered.
  • Search the archives of these forums. Chances are good that somebody has run into the same issue before.
  • Do Not Report Bugs on these forums. Bugs and feature requests should be logged on the ISSUES LIST. The App Inventor team uses the data on the ISSUES LIST to inform its development priorities.
  • Be very clear about your question in the subject and give plenty of details in your post to help others understand your problem. Code snippets, screen shots, source files for your app may also be helpful.
